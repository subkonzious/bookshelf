The Furies of Calderon - Jim Butcher


"Logic is a fury all its own." - Fidelias, The Furies of Calderon, Chapter 7 - Jim Butcher

"Tavi would remember that walk for the remainder of his life, as the torment a starving mouse must feel when darting between the feet of giants to snatch at crumbs of food and then rush back to safety." (Chapter 10) I consider this quote noteworthy because it provokes an empathetic understanding of a non-human creature. While empathy is often considered to be a human-to-human emotion, this line in the story does an excellent job of expanding one's empathy outward to non-human beings.
